# Artificial Intelligence
![Many everyday gadgets that people use incorporate AI.](https://www.nibib.nih.gov/sites/default/files/inline-images/AI%20600%20x%20400.jpg)

<span style= "font-family:Papyrus; font-size:1em;">Artificial intelligence (AI) is the replication of human intellect in devices that have been designed to reason and acquire knowledge similarly to humans. Robotics, machine learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and other topics fall under the umbrella of artificial intelligence (AI).

## History
* 1956: The term "Artificial Intelligence" is coined at the Dartmouth Conference.
* 1997: IBM's Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Garry Kasparov.
* 2011: IBM's Watson wins the game show Jeopardy! against human competitors.

## Applications
* Customer Service: Chatbots and virtual assistants provide automated customer support.
* Education: AI-based tools enhance personalized learning and adaptive tutoring.

## Challenges and Concerns
### While AI offers significant advancements, it also poses challenges and concerns
* Job Displacement: AI-enabled automation might result in workforce changes and job losses.
